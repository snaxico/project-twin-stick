## Design Decisions Made

### Economy
- **Gold** is the single currency. Enemies drop gold on kill.
- Gold does not decay. Magnet pull radius for pickup. Auto-collect on room clear.
- **No free mutations.** All mutation picks cost gold. 0 gold = 0 mutations.
- Passive play is soft-punished: no gold = no mutations = death spiral over 3-4 rooms.
- No hard kill gate. Survive = room clear regardless of kills.

### Encounter Model
- **Survive** is the universal room goal. No competing primary objectives.
- Room variety comes from encounter type + enemy package + modifiers + anchor threats.
- Four node types on map: Combat, Elite, Rest, Boss.
- Hazards (fire floor, ice zones, etc.) are room modifiers, not a separate node type.
- Encounters are authored combinations, not random rolls.
- Harder encounters naturally yield more gold through tougher enemies.

### Progression
- Room-end: spend gold on random mutation picks (1 of 3). Cost scales per pick (30 / 60 / 100 placeholder).
- Shops: curated mutations + healing + rerolls + consumables. Also cost gold.
- Room-end = steady power. Shops = build-shaping + support.
- Run must not depend entirely on shops.

### Abilities
- Abilities (weapon, primary skill, secondary skill) are separate from mutations.
- Current prototype uses fixed loadout: Rifle + Shockwave + Dash.
- Ability acquisition: pre-run loadout from meta-unlocked options (Option A).
- Fallback: hybrid in-run shop acquisition (Option C) if playtesting reveals pre-run is too static.

### Rejected Directions
- Hold Zone as a primary objective (conflicts with kill economy).
- Pure kills = XP = level-up (too generic, invites invisible stat soup).
- Mid-combat pause for upgrade selection (breaks flow).
- Ability cooldown tied to energy collection (too complex for non-gamers, exploitable).
- Temporary power core pickups (fights with room-end mutations, hard to balance).
- Hard kill gates to clear rooms (unnecessary with gold economy).

## Design Review Fixes

Applied after external review feedback:

- **Death spiral prevention:** Added 20g flat survival bonus per room clear. Prevents zero-progression walls after bad rooms.
- **Spending hierarchy:** Room-end picks are cheap (15/50/100g) and primary. Shop items are expensive (80-150g) and secondary. Prevents hoarding trap.
- **Co-op wallet model:** Shared pickups, personal wallets. When anyone collects gold, ALL players get the full amount. No stealing, no routing arguments.
- **Shop node type:** Added Shop as a formal 5th node type (Combat/Elite/Rest/Shop/Boss). At least one Shop guaranteed reachable per run.
- **Ability acquisition wording:** Softened from "Current decision" to "Prototype direction, not locked."
- **Finite spawn budget:** Documented that 60s timer caps gold farming. Rooms have predictable gold yield by depth.
